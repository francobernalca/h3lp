You don't have to figure this out alone.

h3lp is a friendly, free hub that connects you to real, verified help across Ontario. Whoever you are and whatever you're going through, there's something here for you. No sign-up, no judgement.

Free and open to everyone, any age or background 🍁

Where are you?

About these links. h3lp is a free, non-profit signpost for Ontario. We don't run any of these services. We point you to trusted Ontario and Canada-wide organizations so you can reach the real help directly. The province-wide lines (988, 211, 811, Kids Help Phone) work from any city. Local listings were checked when this page was built; if you spot a broken link, please tell us, and confirm any local detail by calling 211.

In immediate danger? Call 911. This site sets no cookies, loads no trackers, and stores nothing about you.
